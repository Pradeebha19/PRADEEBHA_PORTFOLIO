The Blood Bank Management System is a desktop-based application designed to maintain and manage the details of donors, recipients, blood groups, and stock levels of blood units. The system provides a user-friendly interface built using Tkinter (Python's GUI toolkit) and stores all data securely in a Microsoft Access database (.accdb) file. This project aims to digitize and streamline the operations of blood banks, ensuring efficient management and tracking of blood inventory and donor-recipient information.

1. Donor Management:

  • Add new donor details: Name, Age, Gender, Blood Group, Phone Number, Email, Address, Date of Donation.
  • View donor list (with search functionality).
  • Update or delete existing donor records.

    • 2. Recipient Management:

  • Register recipient details: Name, Blood Group Needed, Contact Info, Required Date, Hospital Info.
  • Maintain history of blood issued.
  • Match recipients with available blood units.

    • 3. Blood Inventory Management:

  • Track available stock for each blood group (A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, AB-, O+, O-).
  • Update inventory when new blood is donated or issued.
  • Alert for low stock levels (optional).

    • 4. Search and Filter System:

  • Search donors or recipients by name, blood group, or date.
  • Filter blood availability based on blood group.

    • 5. Reports / Summary:

  • Generate summary reports (e.g., total donors, issued units).
  • View donation history.
  • Daily/Monthly donation logs.
