About Me

Hi,I'm Pradeebha Murugavel

A Data Science graduate with a strong foundation in mathematics, programming, and big data technologies. I hold an MSc in Data Science from the University of Greenwich, London, and a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from Saradha Gangadharan College in Puducherry, India.

My career began in education, where I served as an Academic Trainer and later as a Curriculum Executive. During this time, I taught programming, data analysis, and IT skills, while also developing and refining technical training materials to align with industry trends. These roles strengthened my communication, curriculum design, and hands-on teaching abilities — and deepened my passion for making complex concepts accessible to others.

Technically, I’m fluent in Python, R, and SQL, with practical experience in libraries and tools such as Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, XGBoost, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. I also bring cloud experience with Microsoft Azure, and I’m comfortable working with big data frameworks like Hadoop, Spark, and Hive. My visualization skills extend to platforms like Tableau, and I’ve applied these tools in projects such as product matching systems, payroll automation, and blood bank data management.

I’m professionally fluent in English and conversational in French, with a Diploma in French Language and additional certifications in Python Programming and Computer Applications.

I’m passionate about the intersection of education and technology, and I thrive in roles where I can combine data-driven insight, structured problem solving, and creative thinking to drive real-world impact. Whether I’m building machine learning models, analyzing large datasets, or designing a new learning module, I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to continuously grow.
